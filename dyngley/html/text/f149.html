﻿Virgo coold and drye. Whan that the mone is in the signe of virgo that is of a mayden. that signe is co- monn colde. and drie of kynde and malencolie of kynde of that erthe a good tyme to Wedden a Wyf. not a maide. but so bytwene oon & other and forto sende lettres & charters and messangers and forto sowe & to erye. and forto go by dyverse londes In that signe it is good to eschewe forto leene. forto bathen. & forto putte shippes in to the water either forto go to batel ne no medicyn forto make forto be used for the inwardes of thy body. Libra hoot and moist. Whan the mone is in libra that is a balannce that signe is movable. hoot & moist.
