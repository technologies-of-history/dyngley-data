& setts.


Now  see it is to be vnderstand that in the daye 
In the nyght be n these in humours commyng 
In eny bodye, for a daye materiall is of vj houses.
But to deuyne the daye & the nyght
In my practise to conscerne the compleccion 
Such her houses. To deuyne the daye & the 
Nyght in my practise that it be iiij tymes
Fyrst the blood hath ij houses. And coler
Hath ij houses. So hath fleume and so 
Hath also melencolye. 


Blood regneth two ij houses after mydnyght
Til it be iiij of the clocke, for then the skye blood
Is hotte & moyste so it is part of the daye house work. 


And two ij before none vnto iiij of the Eve
After none regneth coler & it is hotte & drye so
Is that partie of the daye


And two ij of the floor after none til vj of the  clocke
Before mydnyght regneth melencolie & it is colde 
& drye so is it partie of the nyght cold & drye
