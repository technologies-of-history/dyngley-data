					<title>for the ache of disease in the raynes of the bakke</title>
				</head>
				<p>Take a <measure>quarter of yard</measure> of redde scarlett &amp; stycche therto the woll of a blakke shepe thatt growyth apon the brest and then take pouder of [?]aunders &amp; strew over the sayd woll &amp; also pouder of lycoras and after that don to over <rs select="#rs-proc">spryngyll</rs> the same with <rs select="#rs-ing">oyle of oylyff</rs> and that plastur shall restore nature. And ease the pacient of his disease so it be layed to his raynes &amp; 
            <measure>every wyke</measure> the same plastre to be renewed with same pouder &amp; oyle. And so to use it till he be hoole &amp;c.</p>
