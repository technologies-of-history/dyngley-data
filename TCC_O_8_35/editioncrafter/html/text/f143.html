Malencolye folke ben best in ver. wel in somer & in wynter. and worst in hervest. Eest These ben the Southe foure wyndes Weste Northe Eest Wynde. is hoot & moist. Southe Wynde. is hoot & drye. West Wynde. is colde and moist. Northe Wynde. is colde and drie. Colerike folke ben wel. while the sonne or the wynde be eest. west & north. but evel thei ben while the sonne or the wynde are southe Sangweynn folke ben evel. while the sonne or the wynde is in the eest. but thei ben wel while the wynde or the sonne is in the sowthe and in the weste. or in the northe.
