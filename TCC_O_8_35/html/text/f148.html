Leo. hoot and dryr Whan the mone is in leone. whiche signe is stedfast and stable. hoot and drye and colerike of that kynde of the fire. than is tyme good forto speke with gret lordes and maistres. forto take a trewe feloushippe and also to leene good. and forto bathen. and also to aske dette. Eschewe thou that tyme forto putte shyppes in to the water. either forto drynke eny medycyne for congelynge.
