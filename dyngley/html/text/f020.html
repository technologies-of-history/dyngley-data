<title>Another for the flees.</title><p>Take the horne of a bole & brenne therof in the fyre so þat it smoke and the flees wol fle oute of the house <title>Another.</title> Take ruchous? grece & grese wel a yerde therwith. and stike that yerde in þe chambir flore there as the flees ben. and all the flees that ben in the chamber wol lepe upon that yerde.</p><head><title>For scalled hedes.</title></head><p>Take boles pissynge. & do therto verdegrece and coperose & sayme or bacon grece. & menge hem all togidir and anoynte the heed þer with every morwe. and every evene. & Wasshe the heed with rennyng water & he shal be hool. <title>A nother.</title> Take and shave the pilled hede. than take virgyn waxe & picche of eche iliche muche & boyle hem togidre and sprede a lynnen clothe upon a borde & do this plastir upon that clothe With a selice thynne & lay it hoot on the heed that it hele the scalles & do it not a way in <measure>ix days</measure>. & afterwarde anoynte the heed eche day with hony & it shal hele faire & wol</p>
