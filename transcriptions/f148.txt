
<p>mevyng and sterynge as Weater for soth he is
colde and moist. fleimnitiher of kynde of that Weater
that time is good to entre into newe housing
and forto chainge from house to house. and
from place to place and forto putte shippeb
to winter. and forto wiende in to bataile.
and forto take medycyne. in that signe
thou shalt esthewe to take in wiyt miyde and 
to clepe with hyr<p>
<head>Leo. hoot and dryr<head>
<p>Whan the moine is in leone. wihiche
signe is stedfaft and stable. hoot and drye and 
colerik of that. kynde of the fire. than is tyme
good forto speke with gret lords and milfreb
forto take a trewe feloushuppe and also to
leene good. and forto bathen and also to
aske dette. esthewe thou that tyme forto
drynke eny medycyne forcongelynge.<p>
