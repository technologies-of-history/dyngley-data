For broken hedes of eny wepen falle or hurte. If the sculle or brayn panne be broke or hurte so that the pacient may not speke stampe vyolet & ȝeve it hym to drinke in wyne and if the right side of the heed be hurte. stampe violet & bynde it to the sool of the lifte foot and if it be the lifte side ley it to the sool of the right foot. And than the boon of the heed that is hurte wol arise up in to his place and so than the pacient shall speke. For a stroke in the heed where thorugh the heed is swollen.
