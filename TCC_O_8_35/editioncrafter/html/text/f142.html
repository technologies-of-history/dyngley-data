Malencoly folke thei ben wel in childhood malencolicus and in youthe and evel in olde age. As there ben iiij Ages accordynge to the iiij humoures of complexions right so there ben iiij tymes of the yere and iiij manere of wyndes And iiij parties of the worlde the whiche ben in temperure or malice accordynge to every com- plexionn before saide Ver These ben the iiij Somer tymes of the yere Hervest Wynter Ver is hoot and moist Somer is hoot and drye Hervest is colde & drye Wynter is colde & moist Colerike folke ben wel in ver and in somer. in hervest and in wynter. Sangweynn folke they ben evel in veer & better in somer & wel atte ese in hervest & in wynter Fleumatyke folke ben wel in ver & in her- vest and best in somer & worst thei ben in wynter
