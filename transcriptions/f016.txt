				<p>Here begynneth a tretys of al manere of infirmitees of mannys body. bothe withinne as touchyng to phisyke and withoute as touching to surgerie from the croune of the heed to the sool of the foot. And the remedies therwith if god wol. And furst we wol at heer of the hed how it shulde be norisshede &amp; kepte &amp;c.</p>
