<head>Geminis hoot and moiste.<head />
When that the mone is in Geminis 
that is a signe comon hoot and moist. Sangweynn 
of kynde of that erthe. In good syne it is than
to lerne sotel science. & crafte. as to write 
other to rede. either to studie thynges that 
longen to regall lordes troumymges In that 
signe is forto efthew to be late blood of thyn
Armes & of thyne bondes. for that signe
geminis makith armes and bondes.
