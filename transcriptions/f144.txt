Now  it is to know. that in the day. &
in the nyght ben these iiij. humours regnyng 
in every body. for a day natural is of xxiiij.
houres. But to departe the day & the nyght
in iiij. parties to conserne the complexions 
with her houres. To departe the day & the 
nyght in iiij. parties that is by iiij. tymes
sixe. the blood hath vi. houres. And Coler
hath vi. houres. So hath fleume. and so 
hath also malencolye. 
Blood regnith fro iij. houres after mydnyght
til it be ix of þe kloc afore noon. for why blood
is hoot & moist. so is þat part of þe day hoot & moist. 
And fro ix before none vnto iij. of the kloc
after none regnith. Coler & þat is hoot & drye so
is that partie of the day.
And fro iij. of þe kloc after none til ix. of þe kloc
before mydnyght regnith Malencolie þat is colde 
& drie. & so is þat partie of þe nyght cold & drye.
